How to identify copied S9 pages

A trust checklist for spotting copied S9 pages, fake support routes, duplicate titles, and risky OTP requests.

Quick answer

A trust checklist for spotting copied S9 pages, fake support routes, duplicate titles, and risky OTP requests.

Checklist

  1. Compare title, H1, update date, and support route.
  2. Watch for copied FAQ with no real account or wallet help.
  3. Never share OTP or password with pages found through comments.
  4. Prefer pages with clear contact, privacy, and risk language.

Why this matters for users

This page keeps one narrow search problem separate from broader download, login, wallet, bonus, or comparison pages. That makes the page easier for users to scan and easier for search engines to classify.

Update rule

Refresh this page when a new screenshot, support answer, error text, version detail, or SERP competitor pattern is available. Do not add unrelated keywords just to make the page longer.
