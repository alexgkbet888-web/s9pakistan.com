Account duplication risk

Public registration guides mention multiple accounts on one device as a possible blocking risk. This page turns that into a practical prevention guide.

Action checklist

  1. Use one account route, one phone number, and one saved recovery method.
  2. Avoid registering repeatedly after OTP or login failures.
  3. Check whether account already exists before creating another profile.
  4. If blocked, document error text and wait before retrying.

FAQ

Should users register before checking the source?

No. Users should check the domain, download route, update date, and visible app details before entering phone, OTP, email, password, or wallet information.

Why does this site avoid unverified earning promise claims?

unverified earning promise language is risky and often unsupported. The content should focus on clear steps, account safety, troubleshooting, and responsible-use notes.
